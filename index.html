what dis?

Hackathing UVic introduces beginner programmers, marketers, and designers to hackathons. It’s meant to encourage people to create a project without the pressure of needing to make something serious. There will be a variety of skill levels attending, from people who have hacked for years to people who've never hacked anything in their lives. Come and create something - anything.

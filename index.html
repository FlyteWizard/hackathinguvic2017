Hackathing UVic introduces beginner programmers to hackathons. It’s meant to encourage programmers to create a project without the pressure of needing to make something serious. There will be a variety of skill levels attending, from people who have programmed for years to people who've never coded anything in their lives. Come and create something - anything.
